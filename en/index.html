About us

Sunny Fruit Production was founded in 2015, producer and exporter of dried fruits and nuts. Nowadays, Sunny Fruit is one of the leading companies in this sphere in Uzbekistan. In a relatively short period of time, the company has dynamically developed in all positions to enter the global market. Thanks to the great teamwork, today Sunny Fruit Production is only one of Uzbek companies that exports nuts and dried fruits directly from Uzbekistan to the EU, the USA and Israel.

 

In the capital city of Uzbekistan, Tashkent, we are located in the most optimal location. Our office and production base are located on the same territory in the amount of more than 1 hectare.

 

We produce and supply natural and certified organic food. Sunny Fruit Production is a stable growing company that now has over 200 employees. We are one of the largest – and the only exporter of organic products in Uzbekistan.

Experience

The processing and export of dried apricots, sultana raisins, and almonds from organic farming is one of our strengths.

 

Our company is certified according to all necessary European and international quality standards. Considerable experience in the sphere of dried fruits, production of large volumes with reproducible high quality, honest partnerships, give us the opportunity to contact customers around the world. At the moment, our clients are mainly located in countries such as Germany, France, the Netherlands, Italy, the Czech Republic, Israel and the USA. Meanwhile, we offer a wide range of quality products at very competitive prices.

 

We know that we need to have an innovative introduction in order to provide our customers.

 

Therefore, we surround ourselves with top employees and, thanks to our experience and knowledge in international trade, as well as the ability to innovate which characterize us, we work every day to achieve ambitious and far-reaching goals.

Quality and technology

In our pursuit of continuous improvement,  we have HACCP and ISO 9001 food safety certifications, we ensure that the products that we export are manufactured to the highest quality standards throughout the whole supply chain. Throughout the entire production process – from cultivation, processing, and to the receipt of goods to the client, a traceability procedure for raw materials and the final product is carried out.

 

Therefore, Sunny Fruit Production is certified according to all the necessary European and international quality standards for the wholesale of organic almonds, dried apricots, raisins, grape leaves and oils exported in bulk from Uzbekistan all over the world.

 

The production process uses advanced technologies, which guarantee the high quality of the goods, without the addition of preservatives, flavors and chemical additives.

The goal of Sunny Fruit Production

Currently, the company has been establishing more and more partnerships with foreign countries, so the main goal of Sunny Fruit Production in the near future – to become a reference supplier at the European and global level and increase the recognition of the country made in Uzbekistan.
